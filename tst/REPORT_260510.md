## Work Done (Sessions 194–200)

### 1. Covering Transformation Definition Fix (§81)

**Problem**: `top1_covering_transformation_on` defined covering transformations as
total functions with no constraint outside E. This made the group inverse axiom
`inv_into E h ∘ h = id` genuinely **unprovable** — `inv_into E h (h e)` is undefined
for `e ∉ E`.

**Fix**: Extended the definition with `∀e. e ∉ E → h e = e` (canonical representatives
that are identity outside the covering space). This is mathematically harmless (every
covering transformation has exactly one such representative) and makes function
composition well-behaved.

**Result**: The covering transformation group inverse axiom (`hinverse`) is now
**fully proved**. This closes a sorry that had been flagged as a genuine definitional
issue.
